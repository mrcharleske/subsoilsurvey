Geophysical Survey Methods

We offer a comprehensive range of geophysical survey methods to meet diverse subsurface investigation requirements. Each method provides unique insights into subsurface conditions and structures.

DC/IP Surveys

Direct Current and Induced Polarization methods for mineral exploration and groundwater investigations.

TTEM Surveys

Time-domain electromagnetic surveys for deep subsurface exploration and conductivity mapping.

Seismic Surveys

Reflection and refraction seismology for structural geology and engineering applications.

GSM Surveys

Ground-penetrating radar and magnetic surveys for shallow subsurface investigations.

Gravimetric Surveys

Gravity measurements for density variations and geological structure mapping.

MRS Surveys

Magnetic Resonance Sounding for direct groundwater detection and characterization.

Integrated Geophysics

Multi-method approaches combining various geophysical techniques for comprehensive analysis.
